Naturopathy uses fasting as the first line of management in treating diseases. It is a procedure whereby a person voluntarily goes without food for a certain time, living mainly on their fat reserve instead.
Fasting helps to stimulate autophagy and detox the system resulting in the deep cleansing of cells and tissues of the body. Fasting is a common practice as a regimen for traditional and cultural reasons.

Zone exercises are specialized sets of exercises to bring about appreciable change in the tone, shape and strength of specific body parts. At RK Nature Cure Home, we plan and teach customized zone exercises to patients based on their clinical assessment. The examples of various zones which are focused are like:

 Pelvis, Legs and Feet 
 Lower Abdomen, Lower Back and Legs
 Upper Abdomen and Middle Back
 Upper Back and Chest

Exercising specifically focusing on the respective zones makes it easy and brings faster results along with the bonus of wide and varied other benefits to health. Some of the exercises taught in the zone exercises are like -

 Superman
 Leg Tuck
 Piston Breathing
 Thoracic Mobilization
 Crunch Toe Touch
 Cross Body Mountain Climber
 Sprint on the spot
